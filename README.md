# onekey-rng

This is me following the gambling onekey tutorial with my own twist. Press Space or O to roll. When you hit a 3, the background goes sky blue until the next roll. When you hit 20 total coins, the background goes bronze, when you hit 50 total coins, the background goes silver, and finally, when you hit 100 coins, the background goes gold.
